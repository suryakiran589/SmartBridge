Mode 02

Gesture Control

Control your entire computer without touching the keyboard or mouse. Navigate, click, scroll, adjust volume — all with hand gestures.

✋Open palm → mouse movement
✌️Peace sign → left click
🖖3 fingers → right click
☝️Index up / Fist → scroll up/down
📸Pinky → screenshot
🔒Shaka → lock screen

Mode 03

Air Keyboard

Type into any application by hovering your finger over a virtual QWERTY keyboard in the air. Pinch to press. No physical keyboard needed.

☝️Index finger → pointer/cursor
🤏Pinch → press hovered key
⌫<< key → backspace
⏱️3s delay → click your target window

Why SignBridge?

Deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals often face communication barriers in everyday situations — hospitals, shops, classrooms — where no interpreter is available. SignBridge solves this with a single webcam and no special hardware, making assistive technology accessible to everyone. Built as a Final Year Project in Computer Vision and Human-Computer Interaction.
